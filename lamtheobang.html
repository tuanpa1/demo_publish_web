Scambled Eggs

Eggs are one of my favorite foods. Here is a recipe deliciouly rich scambled eggd.

Ingrediends

  • 2 eggs
  • 1tbs butter
  • 2tbs cream

Method

  1. Melt butter in a trying pan over a medium heat
  2. Gently mix the eggs and cream in bowl
  3. One butter has melted and cream and eggs
  4. Using a spatula fold the eggs from the edge of the pan to the center every 20 seconds (as if you are making an omelette)
  5. When the eggs are still moist remove from the heat (it will continue to cook on the plate until served)
